FAQ

  • What is a copy editor and what do they do?
    A copy editor is a professional who reviews and revises written material to improve its accuracy, clarity, and readability by checking for errors and inconsistencies. They work to make sure the final text is polished, error-free, and easy to understand.
  • How do I know if I need a copy editor?
    Any written material, be it a manuscript, blog post, brochure, report, research paper, or website, can benefit from the expertise of a copy editor. If you find yourself uncertain of the grammatical accuracy, punctuation, spelling, syntax, or consistency of your text, or if you question the readability or effectiveness of your message, a copy editor can assist you in revising and improving your work.
  • How do you charge for your services?
    Copy editors charge by the hour, by the word, by the page (assuming 250 words per page or the standard double-spaced 12-pt font with 1-inch margins), or by the total word count.
  • How do you ensure that my voice and style are preserved in the editing process?
    As professional copy editors, we understand the importance of preserving your voice and style in the editing process. We work closely with you to understand your goals and preferences, provide comments and suggestions in line with your style and voice, and provide a detailed style sheet to maintain your voice and style in future projects.
  • Can you work on a tight deadline?
    Yes! We are able to work on tight deadlines and deliver high-quality work in a timely manner. Let us know the deadline and scope of the project and I will confirm if it is doable and provide an estimated delivery time.

Simply fill out our consultation form and one of our experienced copy editors will be in touch with you shortly to discuss your project and provide you with an accurate quote.   • Do you provide proofreading services?
    Absolutely, we do provide proofreading services! Our team of experienced proofreaders are dedicated to ensuring that your text is accurate, clear and easy to read. We will review your text carefully, checking for spelling, grammar, punctuation, and consistency errors. We'll also check for consistency in formatting and referencing. Our goal is to make sure that your writing is polished and ready to be presented to your target audience. We're here to help you to achieve your goal and make sure that your message stands out.
